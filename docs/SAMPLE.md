## Advanced LaTeX Examples

### Fractions and Roots

Simple fraction: $\frac{1}{2}$

Complex fraction: $\frac{a + b}{c + d}$

Square root: $\sqrt{2}$

Nth root: $\sqrt[n]{x}$

### Subscripts and Superscripts

Subscript: $x_1, x_2, x_3$

Superscript: $x^2, x^3, x^n$

Both: $x_i^2$

### Calculus Notation

Limit: $\lim_{x \to \infty} f(x)$

Derivative: $\frac{dy}{dx}$

Partial derivative: $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$

Integral: $\int_0^1 x^2 dx$

### Linear Algebra

Vector: $\vec{v} = \langle x, y, z \rangle$

Dot product: $\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}$

Cross product: $\vec{a} \times \vec{b}$

Norm: $\|\vec{v}\|$

### Set Theory

Element of: $x \in A$

Subset: $A \subseteq B$

Union: $A \cup B$

Intersection: $A \cap B$

Empty set: $\emptyset$

### Logic

And: $p \land q$

Or: $p \lor q$

Not: $\neg p$

Implies: $p \rightarrow q$

If and only if: $p \leftrightarrow q$

For all: $\forall x$

Exists: $\exists x$
